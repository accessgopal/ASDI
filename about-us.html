ABOUT US

We, the team at ASDI, are a group of professionals who have years of consulting experience in different aspects of actuarial domain. Experiencing the gap between the theoretical knowledge and its application, we have an aim of promoting education by not only focusing on theoretical knowledge/material, but also by training students on the practical application of these theoretical concepts, which is a real test/requirement in any profession.

A key gap that we have noticed in the demand – supply mismatch of jobs, is the level of practical approach of students. This basic underlying need of all recruiters is what we are trying to address through our approach. We train candidates on actuarial concepts, complementing it with a practical approach to ensure that candidates have a balanced approach towards the work that they are required to do in the industry. This mix of theory and practice makes a successful actuary.

Keeping this theory in mind, we treat everybody as participants, rather than students, as we believe that this practical learning process is a collaborative effort and hence, everybody has to be an equal participant in the holistic learning process.
